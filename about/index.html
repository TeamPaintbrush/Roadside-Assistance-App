Our Mission

Our Values

What We Stand For

These aren't aspirations on a poster. They're the principles that drive every product decision we make.

Speed Without Compromise

Every second matters in a roadside emergency. We obsess over response times because we know the difference between 15 minutes and 45 minutes is a customer who trusts us forever — or never calls again.

Radical Transparency

No surprises. Every customer knows who's coming, when they'll arrive, and what it costs before they confirm. Our goal is zero moments of 'I wonder what's going on.'

Technician-First Culture

Our platform works great for customers because it works great for technicians first. Happy, well-equipped technicians deliver better service. We invest heavily in their tools, training, and working conditions.

Technology in Service of People

AI and automation are powerful, but they're tools — not the point. We build technology to make human connections faster and better, not to replace them.

Accessible for Everyone

Roadside emergencies don't only happen in major cities. We're committed to building coverage in rural and underserved areas where traditional providers don't go.

Trust, Earned Every Call

Trust isn't given — it's built one service call at a time. Every rating, every review, every technician credential we verify is part of a long-term commitment to a platform people can count on.
